Alan Frisbee is an enigma of the analog age. Born (allegedly) in a laundromat in Peoria, Illinois, Alan’s musical journey began with a stolen ukulele and a dream. As frontman for The Pelts, he helped define and destroy several genres from 1977 to 1981, before vanishing from the public eye — only to re-emerge in the '90s with his solo masterpiece Returns.
